# PIRD / RIICTIC — Innovation & R&D Support (Tunisia)

**What it is.** Tunisia's public schemes to co-fund innovation and R&D in
companies, administered around the Ministry of Industry / APII and research
agencies. The **PIRD** (Prime d'Investissement dans la Recherche-Développement)
and related instruments (and ICT-focused programs such as RIICTIC) subsidize a
share of the cost of R&D projects, prototyping, technology upgrading, and
innovation activities undertaken by companies, often in partnership with research
structures. They reduce the cash cost of building technically differentiated
products.

**Who qualifies / when to use.** Tunisian companies undertaking a defined R&D or
innovation project (new product, prototype, technological improvement). Directly
strengthens the Innovation Signal's "technology intensity & barrier to entry"
sub-criterion. Eligibility and the subsidy rate depend on the specific scheme.

**How to apply.** File the R&D/innovation project with APII or the relevant agency,
including the technical plan, partners, and budget; the premium is awarded against
eligible expenditure after evaluation.

**Source.** https://www.tunisieindustrie.nat.tn ; https://www.apii.tn
