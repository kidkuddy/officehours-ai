# Value Proposition & Business Model Canvas

**What it is.** Two complementary one-page tools (Strategyzer / Alexander
Osterwalder). The **Value Proposition Canvas** maps customer jobs, pains, and
gains against your product's pain-relievers and gain-creators to test
product-market fit on paper. The **Business Model Canvas** lays out the nine
building blocks of a business (customer segments, value proposition, channels,
relationships, revenue streams, key resources, activities, partners, cost
structure) so the founder can see the whole model and its weak points at a glance.

**Who qualifies / when to use.** Any founder clarifying offer↔need alignment
(Commercial Offer Signal) or sanity-checking the overall model before raising
(Scalability and viability). Particularly useful to expose missing revenue
streams or an unproven channel.

**How to apply.** Fill the Value Proposition Canvas for one customer segment first,
validate the top pains/gains with real interviews, then place it inside a Business
Model Canvas and stress-test each block for evidence vs assumption.

**Source.** Strategyzer — https://www.strategyzer.com/canvas
