# Carthage Business Angels (CBA) — Angel Investment Network

**What it is.** Carthage Business Angels is a Tunisian network of private
individual investors (business angels) who invest their own money in early-stage
startups, typically at the seed stage with tickets ranging from a few tens of
thousands up to a few hundred thousand TND, often syndicated across several
angels. Beyond capital, members provide mentoring, sector expertise, and access
to their networks. Angel rounds in Tunisia frequently combine with the Startup
Act guarantee to de-risk the investment.

**Who qualifies.** Early-stage startups with a working prototype or early traction,
a committed team, and a clear scalability story. A clean cap table and willingness
to take a minority equity partner are expected.

**How to apply.** Apply via the network's website or get a warm introduction from
an existing member or partner incubator; selected startups pitch at investor
sessions, after which interested angels conduct due diligence and structure terms.

**Source.** https://www.carthagebusinessangels.com (and the Tunisian angel network ecosystem)
