# AFD — Agence Française de Développement (Tunisia SME & startup financing)

**What it is.** The French Development Agency (AFD) and its private-sector arm
Proparco finance economic development in Tunisia, including lines of credit and
guarantee facilities channelled through Tunisian banks to SMEs and startups, plus
programs supporting entrepreneurship, innovation, and the green/energy transition.
AFD funding usually reaches startups **indirectly** via partner financial
institutions or co-funded programs rather than as a direct grant.

**Who qualifies.** Tunisian SMEs and startups eligible under the specific AFD
credit line or program (often tied to sectors such as green economy, digital,
agriculture, or job creation). Eligibility is defined per program and per partner
bank.

**How to apply.** Identify the active AFD credit line or program (via the AFD
Tunisia office) and apply **through the partner Tunisian bank or implementing
organization** that distributes it; some thematic calls accept direct
applications.

**Source.** https://www.afd.fr/en/page-region-pays/tunisia
