# EU — Horizon Europe (Tunisia as Associated Country)

**What it is.** Horizon Europe is the European Union's research and innovation
framework programme (2021–2027, ~€95bn). Tunisia is an **associated country**,
meaning Tunisian companies, startups, and research organizations can participate
in collaborative projects and access funding on essentially the same footing as
EU entities. Relevant strands for startups include collaborative innovation
actions and SME-oriented instruments within Pillar III (the European Innovation
ecosystem), as well as thematic calls (digital, green, health, agri-food).

**Who qualifies.** Legal entities established in Tunisia (companies, startups,
universities, research centres). Most calls require a consortium of partners from
several countries; eligibility and the funding rate depend on the specific call
and action type.

**How to apply.** Find open calls on the EU **Funding & Tenders Portal**, join or
build a consortium, and submit the proposal by the call deadline through the
portal. Tunisia's National Contact Points (NCPs) help with partner search.

**Source.** https://ec.europa.eu/info/funding-tenders/opportunities (Funding & Tenders Portal)
