# Smart Capital — Operator of the Startup Act & Startup Tunisia

**What it is.** Smart Capital is the public-private company mandated to operate the
Startup Act and to run the "Startup Tunisia" national initiative. It manages the
ANAVA Fund of Funds, coordinates the Startup Labelisation process, and runs
support programs, intermediation, and ecosystem-development activities (including
international market access and investor connections) for Tunisian startups.

**Who qualifies.** Tunisian startups, especially those pursuing or holding the
Startup Label; founders seeking guidance on Startup-Act benefits, financing, and
ecosystem programs.

**How to apply.** Engage through **Startup.gov.tn** and Smart Capital's programs:
obtain the Startup Label, then access the financing intermediation, support
tracks, and investor introductions Smart Capital coordinates.

**Source.** https://www.smartcapital.tn ; https://startup.gov.tn
