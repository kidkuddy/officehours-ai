# Startup Act — Startup Labelisation (Label Startup)

**What it is.** The Tunisian Startup Act (Law n°2018-20 of 17 April 2018) created
a legal status, the "Startup Label", granting labelled companies a package of
fiscal, social, and financial benefits for up to 8 years. It is the cornerstone
of Tunisia's startup ecosystem and the gateway to most Startup-Act benefits.
Benefits include: a one-year departure-and-return guarantee for founder-employees
("congé pour création de startup"), state coverage of founders' salaries for up to
3 founders for one year ("bourse de startup"), full exemption from corporate
income tax, exemption from the TFP and FOPROLOS payroll contributions, and
simplified customs and foreign-currency rules.

**Who qualifies.** A company incorporated under Tunisian law, less than 8 years
old, with fewer than 100 employees, majority-owned by individuals/founders or
investment vehicles, that is innovative and has high scalability potential. The
label is awarded by a "Collège de Labellisation" against an innovation and
scalability rubric.

**How to apply.** Apply online through the official portal **Startup.gov.tn**:
create a file, submit the pitch deck, business plan, and incorporation documents,
and the Collège reviews on a rolling basis (decisions typically within ~weeks).

**Source.** https://startup.gov.tn — Startup Act, Law n°2018-20.
