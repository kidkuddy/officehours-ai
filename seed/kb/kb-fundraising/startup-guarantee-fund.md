# Startup Guarantee Fund (Fonds de Garantie des Startups)

**What it is.** A guarantee mechanism created under the Startup Act and managed in
connection with the SOTUGAR / Caisse des Dépôts et Consignations (CDC) ecosystem.
It guarantees equity investments made into labelled startups, covering a large
share of an investor's loss if the startup fails. By de-risking early-stage
equity, it is designed to mobilize private VC and SICAR capital into Tunisian
startups that would otherwise be considered too risky.

**Who qualifies.** Companies holding the Startup Label under the Startup Act.
The guarantee is triggered for eligible equity investments made by approved
investment vehicles (SICAR, FCPR, business angels) into those startups.

**How to apply.** The startup must first obtain the Startup Label via
Startup.gov.tn. The guarantee is then arranged by the investing vehicle through
the managing institution (CDC) as part of structuring the investment round.

**Source.** https://startup.gov.tn ; https://www.cdc.tn — Startup Act guarantee fund.
