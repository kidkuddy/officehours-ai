# UNDP Tunisia — Accelerator Lab & Innovation Funding

**What it is.** The United Nations Development Programme (UNDP) in Tunisia runs an
Accelerator Lab and a portfolio of innovation and entrepreneurship initiatives
that provide grants, challenge prizes, capacity-building, and pilot funding to
startups and social enterprises addressing development challenges (jobs, climate,
inclusion, the SDGs). Support is usually delivered through time-bound calls,
challenges, and partnerships rather than a standing fund.

**Who qualifies.** Startups, social enterprises, NGOs, and innovators whose
solution addresses an SDG-aligned challenge defined by the specific call — often
with a focus on youth employment, green transition, marginalized regions, or
social impact.

**How to apply.** Watch for open calls and challenges announced on UNDP Tunisia's
channels and partner platforms, then submit the project against the call's
criteria and timeline.

**Source.** https://www.undp.org/tunisia
