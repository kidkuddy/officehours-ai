# BTS — Banque Tunisienne de Solidarité

**What it is.** A microfinance-oriented state bank that provides small loans to
entrepreneurs, micro-enterprises, graduates, and craftspeople who lack access to
traditional bank credit and collateral. BTS funds small projects (from a few
thousand TND up to ~150,000 TND depending on the scheme) at favourable rates,
including micro-credit through its network of associations, and is a common first
financing step for very early micro-entrepreneurs.

**Who qualifies.** Tunisian nationals with a small, viable project — often new
graduates, artisans, small farmers, and micro-enterprise promoters — who can
demonstrate the project's feasibility. Minimal personal contribution is required;
the emphasis is on financial inclusion rather than large collateral.

**How to apply.** Apply at a BTS branch or through a partner micro-credit
association: present the project, an estimate of needs, and supporting documents;
BTS assesses feasibility and disburses against the approved plan.

**Source.** https://www.bts.com.tn
