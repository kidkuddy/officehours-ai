# ANAVA — Fund of Funds for Tunisian Startups

**What it is.** ANAVA is Tunisia's "Fund of Funds", set up by the State with
support from the World Bank, the German KfW, the European Investment Bank, and
Caisse des Dépôts et Consignations (CDC), and managed via the Startup-Act
ecosystem. It does not invest directly in startups; instead it invests in
**private venture-capital funds** (SICARs/FCPRs) on the condition that they
finance Tunisian startups, multiplying the private capital available to the
ecosystem across seed and early-growth stages.

**Who qualifies.** Indirectly, labelled and early-stage Tunisian startups that
raise from the VC funds ANAVA backs. Directly, the beneficiaries are the fund
managers selected by ANAVA.

**How to apply.** Startups access ANAVA capital by raising from the **partner VC
funds** it has invested in — identify those funds (often via Smart Capital / the
Startup Act network) and pitch them directly. Fund managers apply to ANAVA
separately.

**Source.** https://www.smartcapital.tn ; https://startup.gov.tn
