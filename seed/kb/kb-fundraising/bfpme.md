# BFPME — Banque de Financement des Petites et Moyennes Entreprises

**What it is.** A state-owned development bank dedicated to financing the creation
and expansion of Tunisian SMEs. BFPME co-finances investment projects (typically
from ~100,000 TND up to several million TND) alongside commercial banks, often
combined with a SOTUGAR guarantee, and provides project-structuring support. It
specializes in projects that commercial banks find too risky to fund alone.

**Who qualifies.** SMEs and project promoters with a viable investment project in
industry, services, or technology — usually requiring the promoter to bring a
minimum equity contribution (commonly ~30% of the project cost) and a bankable
business plan. Both new ventures and expansions are eligible.

**How to apply.** Submit a complete financing file (business plan, financial
projections, legal and promoter documents) to a BFPME regional office or via the
website; BFPME analyzes the project, structures the financing with co-lenders,
and presents it to its credit committee.

**Source.** https://www.bfpme.com.tn
