# SOTUGAR — Société Tunisienne de Garantie

**What it is.** Tunisia's national credit-guarantee company. SOTUGAR guarantees a
share (commonly up to 75% for eligible categories) of bank loans and leasing
extended to SMEs and project promoters, so that a lack of collateral does not
block otherwise-viable projects. It manages several guarantee funds, including
funds dedicated to SMEs and to investments under the investment-incentive code.

**Who qualifies.** SMEs and promoters financing eligible investment projects
(creation or expansion) through a partner bank or leasing company. Eligibility and
the guarantee rate depend on the specific fund and sector.

**How to apply.** The guarantee is arranged by the **financing bank**, not the
entrepreneur directly: when the bank approves a loan, it requests the SOTUGAR
guarantee on the borrower's behalf as part of the credit file.

**Source.** https://www.sotugar.com.tn
