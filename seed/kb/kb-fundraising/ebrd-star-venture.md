# EBRD Star Venture & Advice for Small Businesses (Tunisia)

**What it is.** The European Bank for Reconstruction and Development (EBRD)
operates in Tunisia with two programs relevant to founders: **Star Venture**,
which gives high-potential early-stage startups intensive mentoring, advisory
grants, and investor access; and **Advice for Small Businesses (ASB)**, which
co-funds consultancy projects (strategy, marketing, operations, digitalization)
for established SMEs. EBRD also provides direct and intermediated debt/equity to
larger SMEs.

**Who qualifies.** Star Venture targets innovative, scalable early-stage startups.
ASB targets existing SMEs (typically with some operating history and revenue) that
need a defined advisory project. Eligibility follows EBRD's country and program
criteria.

**How to apply.** Apply through the EBRD Tunisia "Know How" / SME programs portal
or the local EBRD office; selection is by program with a defined application and
scoping process.

**Source.** https://www.ebrd.com/work-with-us/advice-for-small-businesses.html
