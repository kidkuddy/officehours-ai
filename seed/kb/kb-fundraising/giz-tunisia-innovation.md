# GIZ Tunisia — Entrepreneurship & Innovation Support

**What it is.** GIZ, the German development agency, implements several programs in
Tunisia supporting entrepreneurship, employment, and innovation on behalf of the
German government and the EU — including support to incubators, startup
competitions, skills programs, and the digital and green economy. GIZ typically
provides non-financial support (training, coaching, ecosystem building) and
co-funds challenges and grant facilities rather than investing as equity.

**Who qualifies.** Startups, SMEs, incubators, and ecosystem actors targeted by a
specific GIZ program — frequently focused on job creation for youth, regional
development, digitalization, or the green transition.

**How to apply.** Engage via the relevant active GIZ program or its implementing
partner; participation is usually through program-specific calls, cohorts, or
partner incubators rather than direct individual applications.

**Source.** https://www.giz.de/en/worldwide/325.html (GIZ Tunisia)
