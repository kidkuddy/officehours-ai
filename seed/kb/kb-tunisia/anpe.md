# ANPE — Agence Nationale pour l'Emploi et le Travail Indépendant (ANETI)

**What it is.** Tunisia's national employment agency (commonly referred to as ANPE
/ ANETI). It runs a portfolio of programs supporting job creation and self-
employment, including support for entrepreneurs and self-employed promoters:
entrepreneurship training (e.g. CEFE/MORAINE-style programs), the "Forsa"-type
self-employment support, accompaniment to set up micro and small projects, and
links to micro-financing. It is the main public channel for employment-linked
entrepreneurship support, especially for young graduates and job seekers.

**Who qualifies.** Job seekers, young graduates, and aspiring self-employed
promoters seeking to create their own small business or micro-project. Eligibility
depends on the specific program (age, employment status, project type).

**How to apply.** Register and apply at a local ANETI employment office or through
its online services; advisors guide candidates into the appropriate
entrepreneurship-support or self-employment track.

**Source.** https://www.emploi.nat.tn (ANETI)
