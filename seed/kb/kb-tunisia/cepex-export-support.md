# CEPEX — Centre de Promotion des Exportations

**What it is.** Tunisia's export-promotion centre, under the Ministry of Trade.
CEPEX helps Tunisian companies access foreign markets through market intelligence,
participation in international trade fairs, B2B matchmaking, export coaching, and
co-financing of export-development activities via the **FOPRODEX** export-
development fund (which can cover a share of costs for promotion, fairs, and
market prospecting abroad). For a startup ready to sell beyond Tunisia, CEPEX is
the main public market-access partner.

**Who qualifies.** Tunisian companies with an exportable product or service that
want to develop or expand international sales. FOPRODEX support has its own
eligibility and co-financing rules per activity.

**How to apply.** Contact CEPEX or apply through its services and the FOPRODEX
scheme for a specific export action (fair, mission, market study); submit the
activity plan and supporting documents.

**Source.** https://www.cepex.nat.tn
