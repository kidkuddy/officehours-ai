# APII — Agence de Promotion de l'Industrie et de l'Innovation

**What it is.** Tunisia's national agency for promoting industry and innovation,
under the Ministry of Industry. APII is the central one-stop institution for
business creation and industrial investment: it runs the online company-creation
portal, hosts business incubators (pépinières d'entreprises) across the country,
manages investment-incentive files, and supports innovation through its innovation
and technology programs. For most industrial or tech founders, APII is the first
public institution they interact with.

**Who qualifies.** Entrepreneurs and SMEs creating or expanding an industrial,
service, or innovative activity in Tunisia. Specific incubation and incentive
schemes have their own criteria (sector, project maturity, region).

**How to apply.** Use the APII online services (company registration, project
filing) at the agency's portal, or approach an APII regional directorate /
pépinière directly to access incubation and advisory services.

**Source.** https://www.tunisieindustrie.nat.tn ; https://www.apii.tn
