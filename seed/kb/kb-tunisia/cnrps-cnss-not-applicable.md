# Investment Incentives Code & FOPRODI (Tunisia)

**What it is.** Beyond the Startup Act, Tunisia's **Investment Law (Law 2016-71)**
and the historic **FOPRODI** (Fonds de Promotion et de Décentralisation
Industrielle) provide investment incentives for project promoters: capital grants
and premiums (notably for projects in regional-development zones and priority
sectors), participation in the promoter's equity via SICARs, and SOTUGAR-backed
financing. The regime favours job creation, regional development, and priority
sectors, and is managed through APII/agencies and the investment authority (TIA).

**Who qualifies.** New promoters and SMEs investing in eligible sectors, with
enhanced benefits for projects located in regional-development areas and for young
first-time promoters meeting the minimum equity requirement.

**How to apply.** File the investment project and incentive request through APII /
the Tunisia Investment Authority (TIA) and the financing bank, which arranges the
FOPRODI participation and any guarantee.

**Source.** https://www.tia.gov.tn ; https://www.tunisieindustrie.nat.tn (FOPRODI)
