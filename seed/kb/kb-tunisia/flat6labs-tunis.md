# Flat6Labs Tunis — Seed Accelerator

**What it is.** Flat6Labs is a leading regional seed-stage accelerator and early-
stage fund operating across MENA, with a Tunis program. It runs cohort-based
accelerator cycles offering seed investment (a defined ticket in exchange for
equity), four-or-so months of intensive mentorship, workspace, and a demo day
connecting startups to follow-on investors. It is one of the most established
private accelerators feeding the Tunisian startup pipeline.

**Who qualifies.** Early-stage startups (typically with an MVP or early traction)
and committed founding teams targeting scalable, tech-enabled markets. Selection
is competitive and cohort-based.

**How to apply.** Apply online when a cohort intake is open via the Flat6Labs
Tunis application page; shortlisted teams go through interviews and a selection
committee before joining the cycle.

**Source.** https://www.flat6labs.com/tunis
