# 216 Capital & Tunisian VC Funds (Seed–Series A)

**What it is.** 216 Capital is a Tunisian venture-capital fund investing in early-
stage, tech-enabled startups (seed to Series A) in Tunisia and the region,
typically alongside the Startup Act guarantee and other VC funds backed by ANAVA.
It is representative of the new generation of Tunisian VC funds (such as those
managed under the Smart Capital / ANAVA ecosystem) that provide priced equity
rounds plus active board-level support to scalable startups.

**Who qualifies.** Incorporated, scalable tech startups with demonstrated traction
and a credible growth and exit story; a clean cap table and the Startup Label
strengthen the case.

**How to apply.** Reach out directly to the fund with a pitch deck, business plan,
and financials, or get a warm introduction via an incubator/accelerator; the round
is then structured and negotiated.

**Source.** https://www.216capital.vc ; https://www.atic.org.tn
