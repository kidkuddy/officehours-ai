# RedStart Tunisie — Incubator & Entrepreneurship Support

**What it is.** RedStart Tunisie is a non-profit incubator and entrepreneurship-
support organization that runs incubation and acceleration programs, training,
mentoring, and competitions for Tunisian founders, often in partnership with
public bodies, banks, and international donors. It focuses on accompanying
entrepreneurs from idea to a structured, financeable project, including projects
in the regions and among youth.

**Who qualifies.** Early- to growth-stage entrepreneurs and project promoters,
including youth and regional founders, depending on the specific program. Each
program defines its sector, stage, and geographic criteria.

**How to apply.** Apply to an open program or call via RedStart's website or its
partner channels; selection is program-based with interviews and committees.

**Source.** https://www.redstart.tn
