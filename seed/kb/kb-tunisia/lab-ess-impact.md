# Lab'ESS — Incubator for Social & Solidarity Economy

**What it is.** Lab'ESS (Laboratoire de l'Économie Sociale et Solidaire), supported
by Tunisian and international partners, is an incubator and support centre for
social enterprises, NGOs, and impact-driven projects in Tunisia. It offers
incubation programs, training, coaching, co-working, and grants/support for
projects with a social or environmental mission, helping them structure their
model and access impact financing.

**Who qualifies.** Social entrepreneurs, associations, cooperatives, and
impact-oriented startups whose primary goal is social or environmental value
(ESS — Économie Sociale et Solidaire). Specific programs have their own intake
criteria.

**How to apply.** Apply to an open Lab'ESS incubation program or call via its
website, or contact the centre to access training and accompaniment services.

**Source.** https://www.labess.tn
