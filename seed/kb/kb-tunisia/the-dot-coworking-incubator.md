# The Dot — Innovation Hub & Incubator

**What it is.** The Dot is a Tunisian innovation hub and incubator providing
co-working space, structured incubation and pre-acceleration programs, training,
events, and mentoring for early-stage founders. It partners with public and
international programs to deliver entrepreneurship support, and acts as a community
anchor connecting founders to mentors, partners, and the broader ecosystem.

**Who qualifies.** Early-stage founders and small teams looking for a structured
environment, mentorship, and community to develop and validate their projects.
Program-specific eligibility applies to each cohort or call.

**How to apply.** Apply to an open program or membership through The Dot's website
or visit the space to learn about current incubation tracks.

**Source.** https://www.thedot.tn
