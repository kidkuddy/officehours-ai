# Wiki Start-up / The Cube — Incubator & Innovation Centre

**What it is.** Wiki Start-up (operator of "The Cube" innovation centre) is one of
Tunisia's pioneering private incubators and innovation enablers. It provides
incubation, acceleration, training, fundraising support, and access to its
investor and mentor network, and has been active in structuring the Tunisian
startup ecosystem and connecting founders to regional and international capital.

**Who qualifies.** Early- and growth-stage Tunisian tech startups seeking
incubation, structuring, and fundraising support. Each program/cohort sets its own
criteria.

**How to apply.** Apply to an open program or request incubation/advisory services
through Wiki Start-up's channels; selection is program-based.

**Source.** https://www.wiki-startup.com
