# Startup Act (Tunisia) — Legal Framework Overview

**What it is.** The Startup Act (Law n°2018-20, with its application decree) is
Tunisia's pioneering legal framework — the first of its kind in Africa — to
encourage startup creation. It defines what a "startup" is, creates the Startup
Label, and bundles benefits across three audiences: for **founders** (a one-year
leave from a public/private job to create a startup, with return guarantee, plus
a state "startup grant" covering salaries for up to 3 founders for a year); for
**startups** (corporate-tax exemption, payroll-contribution exemptions, eased
customs and FX rules, patent-cost coverage); and for **investors** (the Startup
guarantee fund and fiscal incentives on startup investment).

**Who qualifies.** A Tunisian-law company under 8 years old, under 100 employees,
majority held by founders/individuals or investment vehicles, that is innovative
and highly scalable — as assessed by the Labelisation Committee.

**How to apply.** Apply for the Startup Label on **Startup.gov.tn**; once labelled,
the company and its founders unlock the corresponding benefits.

**Source.** https://startup.gov.tn — Loi n°2018-20 du 17 avril 2018.
