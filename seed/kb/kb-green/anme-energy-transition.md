# ANME — Agence Nationale pour la Maîtrise de l'Énergie

**What it is.** Tunisia's national energy-management agency, which administers the
**Fonds de Transition Énergétique (FTE)** — the Energy Transition Fund. It
provides subsidies, premiums, and soft-loan/guarantee mechanisms for energy-
efficiency investments and renewable-energy projects (solar PV, energy audits,
efficient equipment) by companies and individuals. For a startup with an energy or
cleantech angle, ANME/FTE is the primary public lever for the green transition.

**Who qualifies / when to use.** Companies and project promoters investing in
energy efficiency or renewable energy in Tunisia. Strengthens the Green Signal's
"sustainability & SDG alignment" and resource-efficiency evidence. Eligibility and
the support rate depend on the technology and project type.

**How to apply.** Submit the project (often an energy audit first) to ANME under
the relevant FTE scheme; the premium or financing is granted against eligible
investment after technical validation.

**Source.** https://www.anme.tn
