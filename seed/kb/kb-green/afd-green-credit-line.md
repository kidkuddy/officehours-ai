# Green Credit Lines (AFD / EBRD GEFF) in Tunisia

**What it is.** International financiers run dedicated **green credit lines**
channelled through Tunisian banks: AFD's environment/energy lines and the EBRD's
**GEFF** (Green Economy Financing Facility). These provide on-lending to companies
for energy-efficiency, renewable-energy, water, and climate-resilient investments,
often combined with investment grants/incentives and free technical assistance to
size the project. They make green capex cheaper and de-risked for SMEs and
startups.

**Who qualifies / when to use.** Tunisian SMEs and companies financing eligible
green investments (efficient equipment, solar, water savings, sustainable
production). Strengthens the Green Signal and the viability of a cleantech model.
Eligibility follows each facility's technology list and the partner bank's credit
criteria.

**How to apply.** Apply **through a participating Tunisian bank** for the green
credit line; the facility's technical advisers help confirm eligibility and the
associated grant/technical assistance.

**Source.** https://ebrdgeff.com ; https://www.afd.fr/en/page-region-pays/tunisia
