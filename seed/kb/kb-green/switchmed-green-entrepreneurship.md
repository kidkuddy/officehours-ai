# SwitchMed — Green & Circular Economy Support (Mediterranean)

**What it is.** SwitchMed is an EU-funded programme (with UNIDO, UNEP, and partners)
supporting the switch to sustainable consumption and production in southern-
Mediterranean countries including Tunisia. It offers green entrepreneurship
training, coaching, and support for green and circular-economy startups and SMEs,
helping them develop eco-innovative business models and access networks and
follow-on support.

**Who qualifies / when to use.** Green/circular-economy entrepreneurs and SMEs in
Tunisia whose model reduces environmental impact (waste reduction, reuse, resource
efficiency, sustainable production). Directly supports the Green Signal and SDG
alignment. Support is delivered through program calls and cohorts.

**How to apply.** Apply to an open SwitchMed program/call for green entrepreneurs
via its platform or local implementing partners.

**Source.** https://www.switchmed.eu
